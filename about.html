Hi there, I'm James. I design and develop delight and meaning into your user's experience.
LAST UPDATED FEB 2019
I'm a UX Lead, Product Designer, Design Pattern Library creator, Front-End Developer, Seinfeld expert, pizza eater, and traveler.
I love to design, develop, and ask questions. Right now I am the Experience Design Lead for GenAlpha, and specialize in product design, sketching and prototyping, and teaching and emphasizing a user-centered approach to problem solving.
Currently, I'm working on designing and developing 2 new features for an ecommerce product at GenAlpha. An Auto-Reorder feature and a ecommerce flow to purchase courses and classes. Most days are spent designing, meeting with our product owner and COO, discussing how we can build a solution that is intuitive and easy to use.
Some of the things I enjoy that aren't work related? Travel, pizza, and the tri-fecta of TV shows for my generation. "The Office", "Friends", and "Parks and Rec". I love music and play the guitar badly. Staying active has always been a priority of mine, whether that's working out at Shred 4/15 or hiking. At some point in my career I'd like to not only attend talks and conferences but speak about subjects that I'm passionate about.
